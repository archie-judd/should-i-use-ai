Should I Use AI?

A decision flowchart to help you use AI thoughtfully

What's your main goal here?

1. I want to complete a task

Examples:

Building a budget, creating a shopping list, coding a module, organizing documents, generating recipe ideas, troubleshooting technical issues, formatting a presentation
2. I want to ask for advice or feedback

Examples:

Relationship advice, career decisions, style advice, feedback on writing or work, life transitions, parenting decisions, financial planning advice
3. I want to research or learn

Examples:

Exploring philosophical questions, understanding complex topics, developing new skills, academic research, learning about historical events, studying for exams
4. I want to express something personal or creative

Examples:

Creative writing, personal messages to friends/family, artistic projects, social media posts, cover letters, personal statements, heartfelt communications
